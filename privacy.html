Privacy Policy for Growth Trainer

Growth Trainer is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

Information We Collect

When you create an account on Growth Trainer, we collect your email address for account authentication and communication purposes. We do not collect any other personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide it to us voluntarily.

Use of Information

We use your email address to create and authenticate your account, as well as to communicate with you regarding your account, updates to the App, and other relevant information. We do not share your email address with third parties without your consent.

Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and personal data from Growth Trainer at any time. To do so just access to this option on the app under your profile, you will find the option 'Delete account'

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@growthtrainer.fit.
